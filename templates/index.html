OFFLINE SIGNATURE RECOGNITION SYSTEM

To fully utilize the software, it is necessary to follow these instructions. The steps must be completed sequentially to achieve the intended outcome.

1

The first step depends on whether your signature has already been registered in the system or not. If your signature is already registered, click the 'Start' button and choose the batch of users you belong to. If your signature is not registered yet, click the 'Register Signature' button and follow the provided instructions.

2

Once you have selected the batch of users to which your signature belongs, you will proceed to the Reference Page. On the Reference Page, you need to choose your user number, enter the directory or file path of the image you wish to verify, and upload the image for preview. After completing these tasks, click the 'Verify Signature' button to proceed to the final verification stage.

3

After clicking the 'Verify Signature' button, you will be directed to the Result Page, where you can view the verification outcome. The Result Page displays the Accuracy of the Result and the Verification Status of your signature, indicating whether it is Forged or Genuine. By clicking the 'Report Result' button, you can see the verification results of SIFT and the images with their matching keypoints.
